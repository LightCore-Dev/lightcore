Our Story

Two people, one idea, and a name that stuck.

LightCore wasn't planned as a brand. It started as something smaller — an idea, a sound, a name that two people built together and decided was worth sharing.

The Idea

It started as ThatDaylight's idea. She wanted to build something real — a community, not just another page people scroll past.

The Name

The name came from EINLXST3RT<P. He wanted something that felt unique to her server, so he looked at what made sense.

Daylight already had the word Light in it. Core got added because that suffix just hits different in music scenes — it's the part that feels permanent.

What It Became

Put them together and it became something neither of them planned, but both of them meant. Every beat, every collab, every drop since has carried that same idea forward.
